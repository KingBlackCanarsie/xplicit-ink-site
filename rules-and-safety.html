Rules & Safety

Clear expectations. Soft landings. This is how we keep the space respectful, playful, and worth coming back to.

01
Introduction

Start with intention

You’re stepping into a curated space, not a free‑for‑all.

This community is built for connection, creativity, and mutual respect. By being here, you agree to engage with care, read the room, and treat others like real people with real boundaries.

02
Core Principles

Respect, always

No vibe is worth more than someone’s comfort.

Harassment, bigotry, or targeted disrespect are not tolerated. Listen when someone sets a boundary and accept moderation decisions without hostility.

03
General Conduct

Act like you’re in the same room

If it’s out of pocket face‑to‑face, it’s out of pocket here.

Keep conversations on‑topic, avoid spam, and don’t derail spaces meant for others. No doxxing or sharing private content without consent.

04
Consent

Nothing is implied

Interest in chatting is not consent to anything else.

Ask before escalating tone or intimacy. “No,” “stop,” or silence are all answers you respect immediately.

05
Roleplay

Keep it agreed, keep it contained

Every scene is opt‑in, never assumed.

Only engage with people who clearly opt in. Follow channel tags, avoid banned themes, and keep IC and OOC separate.

06
Voice

Use your mic like a guest

Clarity and context matter more than volume.

Don’t scream, blast music, or talk over people. Get consent before recording or streaming calls.

07
Safety

We take harm seriously

If something feels off, say something.

Report threats, stalking, or non‑consensual sharing immediately. Do not retaliate or escalate.

08
Final Notes

Being here is a choice

If this space works, it’s because we keep it that way together.

Staff decisions protect the community. If you’re here, it’s because you help make it better.

These rules may evolve as the community grows. Updates will appear here first.

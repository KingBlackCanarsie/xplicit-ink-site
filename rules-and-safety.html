Rules & Safety

These guidelines keep Xplicit Ink safe, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone.

1. Adults Only
Xplicit Ink is strictly 18+. If staff suspect a member is underage, removal is immediate and permanent.
2. Consent Is Mandatory
No pressure, no guilt-tripping, no ignoring boundaries. “No” is final. Always respect limits and safewords.
3. Respect the Person
Behind every username is a real human being. Treat others with basic decency, kindness, and respect at all times.
4. Privacy Matters
Do not share screenshots, messages, voices, or any content outside the server without explicit permission from all involved parties.
5. Keep Drama Out
No harassment, personal attacks, or dragging issues into public channels. Handle conflicts privately or via staff tickets.
6. Roleplay Is Fiction
Roleplay does not equal real-life consent. Keep IC (in-character) and OOC (out-of-character) clearly separated at all times.
7. Voice Etiquette
No yelling, ear-rape, blasting music, or disrupting others. Match the tone/energy of the voice room you're in.
8. Reporting Issues
If something feels wrong or violates rules, open a private ticket. Staff will review and respond as quickly as possible.

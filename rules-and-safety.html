Rules & Safety

These guidelines keep Xplicit Ink safe, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone.

1. Adults Only
Xplicit Ink is strictly 18+. If staff suspect a member is underage, removal is immediate.
2. Consent Is Mandatory
No pressure, no guilt‑tripping, no ignoring boundaries. “No” is final. Respect all limits.
3. Respect the Person
Behind every username is a real human being. Treat them with basic decency and respect.
4. Privacy Matters
Do not share screenshots, messages, or content outside the server without explicit permission.
5. Keep Drama Out
No harassment, no personal attacks, no dragging issues into public channels. Use staff tickets.
6. Roleplay Is Fiction
Roleplay does not equal real‑life consent. Keep IC and OOC separate at all times.
7. Voice Etiquette
No yelling, blasting music, or disrupting others. Match the tone of the room you're in.
8. Reporting Issues
If something feels wrong, open a private ticket. Staff will respond as soon as possible.

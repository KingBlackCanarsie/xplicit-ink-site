Rules & Safety

A cinematic guide to conduct, consent, and community integrity.

Introduction

Welcome to Xplicit Ink — a community built on intention, maturity, and atmosphere. These rules exist to protect the experience, the people, and the culture we’re creating.

Core Principles

Our foundation is built on respect, consent, clarity, and accountability. Every interaction — written, spoken, or implied — must align with these values.

General Conduct

Members are expected to behave with maturity, respect, and awareness. Harassment, hostility, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

Consent & Boundaries

Consent must be explicit, continuous, and respected at all times.

Roleplay Guidelines
